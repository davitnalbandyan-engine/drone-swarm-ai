# Safety & Use Statement

This project is a **simulation-only autonomous swarm coordination framework**.

## Intended Use
- Research and experimentation
- Education and learning
- Algorithm development and validation in simulation environments

## Explicit Limitations
This software:
- Does NOT control physical drones or vehicles
- Does NOT interface with hardware, flight controllers, or actuators
- Does NOT provide collision avoidance or physical safety guarantees
- Does NOT model real-world physics or sensor noise
